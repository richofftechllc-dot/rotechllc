RICH OFF TECHCoach Guide — Transcribe & scope a call with Gemini

Transcribe a call with Gemini

For any coach — use this when Fireflies wasn't on the call (phone call, in-person, dropped audio, an ad-hoc convo). It turns a raw recording into a clean, scoped summary you can drop into the CRM.

First choice is still Fireflies. Every call booked through the platform auto-records with Fireflies and posts to the CRM. Only reach for this when there was no Fireflies bot on the call.

Step 1 — Capture the audio

  • Phone call: use your phone's call recorder (or speaker + a second device). Save the audio file.
  • In-person / Meet without Fireflies: record with your phone's Voice Memos or the meeting's own record button.
  • Get the file onto your computer (or keep it on your phone if using the Gemini app).

Step 2 — Get the transcript

  1. Open Gemini (gemini.google.com or the app) signed into your Rich Off Tech Google account.
  2. Upload the audio file (paperclip / + button).
  3. First, just get the raw transcript: "Transcribe this call word-for-word. Label each speaker as Coach or Client."
  4. Then run the matching scoping prompt below for the type of call.

Step 3 — Scope it (copy the prompt for the call type)

Paste the audio (or the transcript) plus one of these. Each returns the same CRM-ready shape: summary · action items · grade · follow-up.

Discovery / Intake
You are a Rich Off Tech coaching assistant. From this call recording/transcript, produce: 1) SUMMARY (5-7 sentences): who the client is, where they are in their tech journey, their goal, timeline, and biggest blocker. 2) FIT: which track fits best (ServiceNow CSA, Security+, AWS AI Practitioner) and why, in one line. 3) PLAN: recommend a 30, 60, or 90-day plan (NEVER longer than 90 — we break people into tech fast) based on the hours per week they said they can study. Name the lane and one sentence why. 4) ACTION ITEMS: a checklist of next steps, each tagged [coach] or [client]. 5) MONEY: any pricing, payment, or voucher topics discussed and what was agreed. 6) GRADE (A-F): how likely they are to enroll/start, with one sentence why. 7) FOLLOW-UP: fill in the "Discovery / Intake" follow-up template (below) using this call. Keep it tight and factual. Do not invent anything not in the call.
Coaching / Cert check-in
You are a Rich Off Tech coaching assistant. This is a study/coaching call for a certification client. Produce: 1) SUMMARY (5-7 sentences): what cert/track, where they are in the modules, what was covered today. 2) PROGRESS: domains/topics they're strong on vs. struggling with. 3) PLAN CHECK: confirm or adjust their 30/60/90-day plan based on today's pace and any schedule change they mentioned. NEVER push past 90 days — if they're behind, tighten the load, don't extend. 4) ACTION ITEMS: study tasks before next session, each tagged [coach] or [client], with the quiz domain to drill. 5) BLOCKERS: anything slowing them down (time, access, a confusing topic) + the fix. 6) GRADE (A-F): momentum/exam-readiness, one sentence why. 7) FOLLOW-UP: fill in the "Coaching / Cert" follow-up template (below) using this call. Keep it tight and factual. Do not invent anything not in the call.
Interview Prep
You are a Rich Off Tech interview coach. This was a mock/interview-prep call. Produce: 1) SUMMARY (5-7 sentences): the role they're targeting and how the practice went. 2) STRENGTHS: what landed well (specific answers/stories). 3) GAPS: weak answers, filler, or missing STAR structure — with the exact moment. 4) ACTION ITEMS: drills before next time, each tagged [coach] or [client]. 5) GRADE (A-F): interview-readiness, one sentence why. 6) FOLLOW-UP: fill in the "Interview Prep" follow-up template (below) using this call — include 2-3 questions to rehearse next. Keep it tight and factual. Do not invent anything not in the call.
Clearance
You are a Rich Off Tech clearance-readiness assistant. This call covered the client's security-clearance situation. Produce: 1) SUMMARY (5-7 sentences): their current status, target clearance level, and the path discussed. 2) FLAGS: anything that could affect eligibility that was raised (factual only — do NOT speculate or give legal advice). 3) ACTION ITEMS: documents to gather and steps to take, each tagged [coach] or [client]. 4) TIMELINE: any dates or windows mentioned. 5) GRADE (A-F): how ready/positioned they are, one sentence why. 6) FOLLOW-UP: fill in the "Clearance" follow-up template (below) using this call. Keep it strictly factual. Note where the client should consult an official source rather than us.

Step 4 — Send the follow-up (consistent templates)

Every client gets a same-day follow-up in the same Rich Off Tech voice, no matter which coach ran the call. Gemini drafts it from the call; you tidy the [brackets] and send.

Discovery / Intake — follow-up
Hey [first name] — great talking today. Here's the lane we mapped: • Your goal: [goal, e.g. break into ServiceNow / land a tech role] • Your track: [ServiceNow CSA / Security+ / AWS AI] • Your plan: [30 / 60 / 90]-day — built around the [X hrs/week] you've got • First move: [the one next step] To lock your start: [payment link]. The second that's in, your modules unlock on code [CODE] and I'll connect you with your coach to set the schedule. Anything before you start, just reply. — [coach], Rich Off Tech
Coaching / Cert — follow-up
[first name] — solid session today. Recap: • Covered: [topics / module] • You're strong on: [domain(s)] • We're tightening: [weak domain(s)] • Before next time: [action items] • Plan: still on your [30/60/90]-day track — next exam-ready check [date] Drill [quiz domain] to 85%+ this week and message me if anything gets stuck. — [coach], Rich Off Tech
Interview Prep — follow-up
[first name] — good reps today. What landed: [strengths]. What we're sharpening: [gaps]. Rehearse these before next time: 1) [question 1] 2) [question 2] 3) [question 3] Use the STAR structure on each. You're [grade-level] ready — keep going. — [coach], Rich Off Tech
Clearance — follow-up
[first name] — here's where we landed on clearance: • Target: [level] • Next steps: [documents / actions] • Timeline: [dates discussed] For anything official (eligibility specifics), go through [official source] — I'll keep you moving on everything on our side. — [coach], Rich Off Tech

Step 5 — Put it in the CRM

Take Gemini's output and add it to the client's record in the CRM (Calls tab → add the summary + action items; or turn an action item into a follow-up). This keeps every call — Fireflies or Gemini — in one place.
