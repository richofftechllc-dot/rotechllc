Your ServiceNow CSA Study Guide

The exact path to Certified System Administrator — mapped to your Rich Off Tech course + practice quiz, module by module.

What CSA proves: that you can run the ServiceNow platform — users, roles, data, forms, workflows, and the updates that keep it live. It's your front door into ServiceNow admin, help-desk, and business-process/analyst roles. The exam is taken from home with remote proctoring; your program includes the exam voucher plus a retake voucher.

Step 1 — Set up your lab (PDI)

Before anything else, get your own free ServiceNow instance — a Personal Developer Instance (PDI). This is where you do every hands-on lab. It's free and yours.

  1. Go to developer.servicenow.com and click Sign up (free ServiceNow ID).
  2. Verify your email, sign in, then open the top-right menu → Request Instance.
  3. Pick the latest release when asked. In a minute you'll get your instance URL, admin username, and password — save all three.
  4. Log in. You're now the admin of your own ServiceNow. (Heads up: an unused PDI hibernates after ~10 days — just wake it from the developer site.)
  5. Always study on a desktop/laptop browser, not mobile — the labs need the full interface.

Step 2 — The 8 modules (and where to drill each)

Your course is 8 modules. Each one maps to a domain on your Rich Off Tech practice quiz — study the module, then drill that exact domain until you're scoring 85%+ before you move on.

1

UI & Navigation Quiz: UI & Navigation

The landing page (UI16/Next Experience), application navigator, lists vs. forms, global search, breadcrumbs, favorites, tabs, and the banner. Learn to move fast — everything else sits on top of this.

2

Lists & Filters Quiz: Lists & Filters

List views, the condition builder, sorting/grouping, personalizing columns, list edits, exporting, and dot-walking through reference fields. This is your daily driver as an admin.

3

Forms, Fields & Applications Quiz: Forms, Fields & Applications

Form layout, field types (choice, reference, true/false), mandatory fields, the dictionary, tables & the data model, applications vs. modules, and how records are stored.

4

Flow Designer & Automation Quiz: Flow Designer & Automation

Flow Designer (triggers, actions, flow logic), notifications, SLAs, and the idea of no-code automation. Know when a flow fires and what it can do.

5

Reporting & Dashboards Quiz: Reporting & Dashboards

Building reports (bar, pie, list, trend), sharing them, dashboards, and Performance Analytics at a high level. Turning data into something a manager can read.

6

User Administration Quiz: User Administration

Users, groups, and roles; how access is granted; ACLs at a high level; impersonation for testing. A heavily-tested domain — know how a role flows to a user through a group.

7

ITSM Fundamentals Quiz: ITSM Fundamentals

Incident, Problem, Change, and Request management — the core ServiceNow processes — plus the CMDB and Knowledge basics. This is the "what the platform is actually used for" module.

8

Platform Administration Quiz: Platform Administration

Importing data (import sets & transform maps), update sets, system properties, plugins, and keeping the instance healthy. The "keep it running" admin work.

Step 3 — Pick your pace: 30, 60, or 90 days

Rich Off Tech is a break-into-tech-fast program — no plan runs longer than 90 days. You and your coach pick the lane that fits your schedule, timeline, and goal on your first call. Same 8 modules, same 85%+ target per quiz domain — just a different daily load.

30

30-Day Sprint ~2–3 hrs/day

All-in. For when you have the time now and want to test fast. ~2–3 modules a week, daily quiz drilling, exam at the end of the month.

60

60-Day Standard ~1–1.5 hrs/day

The balanced lane most people take. ~1 module every 5–7 days with room to re-drill weak domains before the exam.

90

90-Day Around-Life ~3–4 hrs/week

The longest we go — built around full-time work and family. ~1 module every 1.5–2 weeks, steady, no burnout. Still certified inside one quarter.

Whatever lane you pick, the rhythm is the same: study the module → drill that quiz domain to 85%+ → next module. Hit 85% across all 8 domains and you're exam-ready. Your coach can adjust your plan anytime your schedule changes — it just never stretches past 90 days.
