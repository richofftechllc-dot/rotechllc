💳 Billing & membership

How does the $27 "2 months" work?

Your first charge of $27 covers your first 2 months (month 2 is free). After that it's $27/mo, cancel anytime. Your access doesn't lapse during the free month.

What's the $227?

$227 for 12 months of founding access, one payment. Best value if you're in for the year — it's $375/year after July 27.

How do I cancel?

Message a coach or reply to your receipt — we'll cancel future billing. You keep access through the time you've already paid for.

Can I split it?

Yes — Afterpay is available at checkout on the bigger items (certs), 4 interest-free payments.

🎓 What's included vs. add-ons

What does founding membership include?

The community, the AI tutors (Bo & Flo), the quiz/study engine (Security+, ServiceNow CSA, AWS AI), weekly community calls, and job drops.

Is 1-on-1 cert coaching included?

No — 1-on-1 cert coaching tracks (Security+, ServiceNow CSA) and clearance guidance are separate paid add-ons on top of your membership. Ask in Discord when you're ready and we'll set you up.

📅 Studying & the plan

How should I study?

Pick a pace — 30, 60, or 90 days — at rotechllc.com/plan. It lays out a dated roadmap through the quiz domains + labs. A coach can set your pace too.

How many hours a day?

Roughly: 30-day ≈ 2–2.5 hrs/day, 60-day ≈ 1 hr/day, 90-day ≈ 40 min/day. Same roadmap, three speeds.

When should I book the exam?

When you're steady at 75–80%+ on practice questions. Book it in the back third of your plan so you have a deadline.

🤝 Referrals

Can I earn by referring people?

If you're a founding member, yes. Share your $127 referral link — when someone joins through it and types your name, you earn $50 (up to $500, or $1,000 in store credit). Payouts process within about 5 business days.
