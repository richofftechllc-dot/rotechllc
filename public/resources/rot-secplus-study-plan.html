CompTIA Security+ — Your Study Plan 🤎

Security+ (SY0-701) is the cert that opens cleared and federal doors — it's on the DoD 8570 baseline. This is the road from zero to pass.

🎯 Why it's the bag

Security+ is the entry credential most cleared IT/cyber jobs list as required. Pair it with a clearance and you're at a $90K–$120K entry point. It proves you understand threats, secure architecture, operations, and governance — the core of every security role.

📚 The 5 exam domains (SY0-701)

DomainWeightWhat it covers
1. General Security Concepts12%CIA triad, controls, zero trust, crypto basics, PKI
2. Threats, Vulnerabilities & Mitigations22%Attack types, malware, social engineering, hardening
3. Security Architecture18%Secure network/cloud design, data protection, resilience
4. Security Operations28%IAM, monitoring, incident response, vuln management
5. Program Management & Oversight20%Governance, risk, compliance (GRC), policies, audits
