CompTIA Security+ — Your Study Plan 🤎

Security+ (SY0-701) is the cert that opens cleared and federal doors — it's on the DoD 8570 baseline. This is the road from zero to pass.

🎯 Why it's the bag

Security+ is the entry credential most cleared IT/cyber jobs list as required. Pair it with a clearance and you're at a $90K–$120K entry point. It proves you understand threats, secure architecture, operations, and governance — the core of every security role.

📚 The 5 exam domains (SY0-701)

DomainWeightWhat it covers
1. General Security Concepts12%CIA triad, controls, zero trust, crypto basics, PKI
2. Threats, Vulnerabilities & Mitigations22%Attack types, malware, social engineering, hardening
3. Security Architecture18%Secure network/cloud design, data protection, resilience
4. Security Operations28%IAM, monitoring, incident response, vuln management
5. Program Management & Oversight20%Governance, risk, compliance (GRC), policies, audits

🗓️ Your 30 / 60 / 90-day plan

Three phases: build the base, secure & operate, then drill & pass. Run acronym flashcards every single day — Sec+ lives on them.

PhaseFocus
Days 1–30
Foundation		Domain 1 — CIA triad, control types, zero trust, crypto + PKI fundamentals. Domain 2 — attack types, malware, social engineering, indicators & mitigations. Start daily acronym drills + easy quiz questions. Goal: you know the threats and the vocabulary cold.
Days 31–60
Architecture & Ops		Domain 3 — secure network/cloud design, data protection, resilience. Domain 4 (the heaviest, 28%) — IAM, SIEM/monitoring, incident response, vulnerability management. Goal: you can reason through how a secure environment is built and run.
Days 61–90
Drill & Pass		Domain 5 — GRC, risk, policies, audits. Then PBQ (performance-based question) drills + full acronym review. Run practice questions in the ROT quiz engine until you're steady at 75%+, schedule the exam in this window, and take it. Goal: booked and passed.

Moving faster? Same order compresses into a 5–6 week sprint if you can put in the hours — the domains just stack tighter.
