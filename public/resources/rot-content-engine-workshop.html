5 — Cost check (know your numbers)

⚠️ Gotchas we learned so you don't have to

  • Claude beats ChatGPT for this. Claude finds the connector instantly, no restart, less hand-holding. ChatGPT needs a restart after installing the MCP and more explicit instructions. If you're new to AI clients — start with Claude.
  • Dubbing runs solo. The auto-dub feature (10 credits/min) wants its own job — don't stack it with clipping instructions in one prompt.
  • Brand template must exist first. The AI can't use a template you haven't created in your Opus Clip account. Make it there once, then it's one word in every future prompt.
  • Long-form works too. It's not just shorts — Opus Clip can cut up to 15-minute horizontal videos from a long stream.
  • Talk, don't type. The mic button in Claude works great — describe what you want out loud and it runs the same job.

🚀 The level-up: your content on autopilot

The trend-jacking recipe: have your AI pull this week's trending topics in your niche → search YOUR past clips for the one that matches a trend → caption it, schedule it, and DM you a receipt. That's riding waves instead of inventing them.
The Monday-morning machine: one folder (or Dropbox) where you drop every long video you record → a scheduled task in Claude that checks it weekly, sends new videos to Opus Clip, captions them, and queues the clips. You wake up to content, done. Record once, post all week.
