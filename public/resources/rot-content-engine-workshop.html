4Gotchas we learned so you don't have to

  • Claude beats ChatGPT for this. Claude finds the connector instantly — no restart, less hand-holding. ChatGPT needs a restart after installing the MCP and more explicit instructions. New to AI clients? Start with Claude.
  • Dubbing runs solo. The auto-dub feature (10 credits/min) wants its own job — don't stack it with clipping instructions in one prompt.
  • Brand template must exist first. The AI can't use a template you haven't created in your Opus Clip account. Make it once, then it's one word in every future prompt.
  • Long-form works too. Not just Shorts — Opus Clip can cut up to 15-minute horizontal videos out of a long stream.
  • Talk, don't type. The mic button in Claude works — say what you want out loud and it runs the same job.
