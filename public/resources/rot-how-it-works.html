📚 How the quiz / study system works

The engine

Log in at rotechllc.com/login → Quiz. It's built by domain for each cert:

Security+ · sp1–sp5 ServiceNow CSA · csa1–csa8 AWS AI Practitioner · ai1–ai4

  • Practice by domain — work one domain at a time; your progress auto-saves.
  • Explanations in plain English — every question tells you why, in Bo's voice, not textbook talk.
  • Aim for 75–80%+ on a domain before you move on. That's your "ready" bar before booking the exam.
  • Labs — for CSA, hands-on labs run against your own free PDI (ServiceNow developer instance). Reading isn't enough — build it.

Your plan (30 / 60 / 90 days)

Go to rotechllc.com/plan, pick your pace, and it lays out a dated roadmap through the domains + labs. A coach can set your pace too. Rough hours: 30-day ≈ 2–2.5 hrs/day, 60-day ≈ 1 hr/day, 90-day ≈ 40 min/day.

The AI tutors

At rotechllc.com/bo: Bo (career + cert coach), Flo (ServiceNow), and the teacher bots. Ask anything, get it explained your way, then have them quiz you back.
